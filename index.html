About Me

I am a software engineer currently attending Parsity, a coding bootcamp that teaches full web stack development. My interest in coding began when I was around 10 or 11 years old and discovered the command line on my dad's Mac. I still remember my amazement at realizing I could operate a computer with lines of code, rather than using a mouse to click on graphics. Shortly after discovering that, I taught myself HTML and began building websites for fun.

After high school, I joined the Colorado Army National Guard and served part time for six years as a Military Police Soldier and a non-commissioned officer. I attended Front Range Community College and Colorado State University for two years until receiving the opportunity to join a small home remodeling business and learn the trades. For the past four and a half years, I have been renovating kitchens and bathrooms, building decks, and painting houses in Northern Colorado.

I built this website to display projects I'm working on, write about coding topics I'm learning about, and encourage other career changers entering the tech field.

Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or send me an email if you'd like to connect.
